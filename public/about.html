Our Mission

At Porsche, we're on a mission to ignite your imagination and fuel your passion for driving. Our mantra, "The only limit is your imagination," is more than just a slogan – it's the driving force behind everything we do. From the iconic 911 to the revolutionary Taycan, each Porsche is crafted with precision and purpose, designed to inspire you to push the boundaries of what's possible. Our mission isn't just about building cars; it's about empowering you to dream bigger, drive farther, and experience the thrill of the open road like never before. So, whether you're carving corners on the track or cruising down the highway, remember: with Porsche, your imagination is the only limit.

Founders & Owners

Ferdinand Porsche / Founder

Ferdinand Porsche founded his engineering office in 1931. Working until his death in 1951, the exceptionally creative technician, formerly a designer of electric cars and racing cars and the chief developer at Daimler-Benz, laid the foundations for the global company that would later bear the same name.

Oliver Blume / Current CEO

Since 1 October 2015 Blume has served as CEO of Volkswagen subsidiary Porsche; the supervisory board of the company appointed him to this position on 30 September 2015. He replaced Matthias Müller, who became CEO of VW Group.
